The problem in our community

MMEA identified the lack of Health and Fitness as a problem. Buffalo City has a sizable student population. However, in order to maintain a healthy lifestyle, these students struggle to find a wide variety of fresh produce from local suppliers. Furthermore, local farmers face challenges in marketing their fresh produce to customers.

The solution

The MMEA Developers are a group of students who have undertaken the responsibility of sourcing fresh produce around the Amathole district farms in the Eastern Cape, making them accessible to the market on a digital platform. 

MMEA Student Delivery

Purchasing fresh produce from farms in the Amathole district of the Eastern Cape. We include potatoes, tomatoes, bell peppers, grapes, bananas, orange sacks, and many other options in our very popular Fruit and Veg Boxes.

MMEA Health & Fitness

A social platform that allows users to interact and share information about various health topics. The platform contains insights from a variety of Buffalo City's athletes, nutritionists, and foodies, who advise users on food, exercise, diet, and nutrition's effects on health.
