About Ruth Dlamini

Mam Ruth Dlamini has been in the food industry for over 25 years. From lunch packs for school kids to full family home cooked meals. Her home is a one-of-a-kind and truly special place, and you can tell that if the walls could talk, they would tell so many stories of community, family, love, good food and history.
